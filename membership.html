Membership

Join a welcoming community of Chihuahua lovers — pet owners, show competitors, rescue advocates, and everyone in between.

Why Join?

The Dominion Chihuahua Club Inc has welcomed Chihuahua lovers since 1964. We embrace pet owners, conformation show dogs, agility and obedience competitors, and rescue dogs with equal enthusiasm.

As a non-profit organisation, your membership fee goes directly toward running events, maintaining the club, and supporting the wider Chihuahua community in New Zealand.

🏅 Members receive discounted entry rates for Championship Shows and access to exclusive Members Only fun days.

How to Join

  1. Email your application to dominionchiclub@gmail.com
  2. Pay the $20 annual fee by electronic bank transfer (details to the right)
  3. Your application is reviewed and approved by the committee
  4. Welcome to the club! 🐾

Membership fees are due 1 April each year. All members must comply with the DCC Constitution and the Dogs New Zealand Code of Ethics.

Governing Documents

All members are bound by two governing documents:

  • The DCC Inc Constitution — the rules that govern how our club operates
  • The Dogs NZ Code of Ethics — standards for responsible dog ownership and breeding

📋 We are actively seeking volunteers and donations to help sustain our non-profit operations. If you'd like to get more involved, please get in touch.

Legal Disclaimer

The Dominion Chihuahua Club Inc and its members operate under the rules and regulations of Dogs New Zealand (NZKC). All advice, events, and activities are conducted in good faith in accordance with those guidelines. The club accepts no liability for individual transactions between members and breeders.
