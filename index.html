About the Club

The Only South Island Chihuahua Specialty Club

Established in 1964 and affiliated with Dogs New Zealand (NZKC), we welcome pet owners, show dogs, agility competitors, and rescues, all with equal enthusiasm.

We are proud supporters of New Zealand Chihuahua Rescue, a charitable trust dedicated to finding loving homes for Chihuahuas in need.
