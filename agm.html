Annual General Meeting

The DCC AGM is held annually and open to all financial members. Elections, motions, and club business are conducted online.

2025 AGM

The 2025 Annual General Meeting ran 13–17 May 2025 online. All financial members were eligible to vote.

✅ The 2025 AGM has now concluded. Minutes and results are available to financial members — contact us at dominionchiclub@gmail.com.

Committee Positions

The following positions are elected at the AGM each year:

  • President
  • Vice President
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Committee Members (up to 4)

Nominations for committee positions must be submitted before the AGM opening date. All nominees must be current financial members of the DCC.

Notices of Motion

Members may submit notices of motion to be voted on at the AGM. Motions must be received in writing before the submission deadline each year.

2024 AGM

The 2024 online AGM ran 22–25 April 2024. Nominations for committee positions were accepted and the meeting concluded successfully. Minutes are available to financial members on request.

2023 AGM

The 2023 online AGM ran 27–30 April 2023. Committee announcements and meeting minutes from this AGM are available to members on request.

How the Online AGM Works

The DCC conducts its AGM online to make it accessible to all members across the South Island and beyond. The process typically runs over several days to allow members in different time zones and circumstances to participate.

  1. Nominations and notices of motion are submitted via email before the AGM opens
  2. The AGM opens and voting papers are distributed to financial members
  3. Members cast their votes over the designated period
  4. Results are announced and minutes distributed
